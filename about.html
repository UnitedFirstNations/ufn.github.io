Our mission is to make a new country that makes life easier for everyone.

United First Nations (UFN) Proposal was built on the idea that a quality, functional government should be available to everyone. Create software, policies, and budgets that eliminate poverty, dissolve borders, creates lasting peace treaties, and fight climate change.
